Events

A list of all camping & astronomical events and planned meetups for the month ahead.

The events below are for everyone unless otherwise stated in the event entry.

August 11 to 13: Peak of Perseids meteor shower

The Perseids are one of the more bighter meteor showers of the year.

They occur every year between July 17th and August 24th and tend to peak around the 9th to 13th of August.

They are made up of debris from the comet Switf-Tuttle and are named after the constellation Perseus as they come from that direction.

At the peak of the shower you could on a very clear night, expect to see around 60 to 100 meteors in a hour form a suitable dark place.

August 20th to 22nd: Perseids End - meetup

Catch up with the end of the Perseid meteor shower ( weather permitting ) and with other members of Adventure works this weekend.

This year the meetup will take place in Elan Valley, Wales and the nearest camp site is at Wyeside.

August 28th to 29th: Camporama Con

This August sees the first annual Camporama Con held at the ExCel London event halls.

There will be various stands covering all major Camping, Caravaning and Motorhome retailers as well as various food and activity stations for campineers younf and old.

Come visit us at our stand and have a chat. See you there.

Membership

Large Network of Sites

As a member you will enjoy priority access to our pitches and events and meetups being held.

We have strict processes in place to help combat the virus and ensure you and your guests remain healthy during your stay.

Discounts

Dedicated Telescope Areas

Our Sites

Camp with us

Camp at one of our many locations we own throughout the UK or enjoy a members only discount we have negotiated for you with some select camp sites scattered across the UK.

We provide pitches for tents, campervans, caravans and motorhomes and most of them come with electrical hook ups.

All our sites are following strict Covid protocols to keep you, your guests and our staff safe. Please adhere to all notices situated throughout the grounds.

Amenities

All our locations have the following amenities as standard.

On arrival at one our locations you are assigned some amenity cards which are allocated to your pitch and allow you to open the various amenity blocks.

Our members get these cards for free as part of your memberships whilst non members are charged a security fee of £10 for 3 cards.

Reception


We operate a 24 hour reception at each location so you are always able to get hold of us as and when you need for any issues or questions you may have.

Face masks must be worn inside reception and only one person is allowed inside at anyone time.

Public Showers


Each site hosts both male and female shower blocks which are cleaned on a regular basis.

Public Toilets


We have both male and female toilet blocks which are cleaned on a regular basis.

Baby Changing Stations


There are two baby changing stations that are cleaned and checked upon on an hourly basis.

Trash & Recycling


There is a central trash bins near the entrance to the site and the recycling points are placed throughout the site.

Every location also has secure areas for you to deposit the waste from your chemcial toilets.

Laundrette


There are 10 washing machines and 10 tumble dryers and a selection of irons and ironing boards.

Tokens for the machines can be purchased from reception.

Shops


The shop stocks the best of the local food, drink and all your cleaning and toiletry requirements as well as some of the bigger name brands as well.
